Frequently Asked Questions

Here are the most frequently asked questions you may check before getting started

What is the duration of the free trial?

Our app is free to try for 14 days, if you want more, you can provide payment details which will extend your trial to 30 days providing you an extra 16 more days to try our app.

What makes Zeraki different from other legal case management apps?

Zeraki is uniquely designed to support both individual lawyers and law firms with tailored features that enhance productivity and communication. For individual lawyers, it offers streamlined case management and direct messaging, while law firms benefit from added functionalities like team collaboration and admin web privileges, all within one mobile application.

What is the storage is for?

Since we provide an extremely detailed reporting and analytics tool, they require quite a bit storage space. For average use, you don’t have to worry about running out of space since the Personal package limits the projects you can have.

For some reason if you run out of space, contact us and we will see what can be done about it and make sure you are not generating unnecessary reports and/or analytics data.

Can I use Zeraki if I am a solo practitioner?

position-absolutely! The Individual Lawyer Plan is specifically designed for solo practitioners. It offers all the essential tools needed for efficient case management, court date alerts, and seamless communication with clients and opposing counsel, directly from your mobile device.

How does the Premium Law Firm Plan enhance team collaboration?

The Premium Law Firm Plan enables law firms to integrate teams within the app, allowing for real-time case assignments, document sharing, and updates. It also includes admin web privileges for managing team settings and department-specific organization, making it easier to oversee larger, more complex legal operations.

Is my data secure with Zeraki?

Yes, security is a top priority for us.
Zeraki uses advanced encryption and security protocols to ensure that all your case information and communications are protected. Additional security features in the Premium Law Firm Plan, such as enhanced access controls, provide even greater security for sensitive information.
